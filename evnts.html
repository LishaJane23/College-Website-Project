College Events

St. Aloysius (Deemed to be University) hosts a wide range of events throughout the academic year that foster community, creativity, and learning. From academic seminars to cultural festivals and sports competitions, these events enrich student life and encourage participation from both undergraduate and postgraduate students...

Cultural Events

Aloysian Fest: A national-level undergraduate fest featuring a variety of competitions and performances...

Sangam: An inter-departmental extravaganza showcasing talents of postgraduate students...

Luminaire: A vibrant French Fest that includes quizzes, fashion shows, open mic, food stalls, and more...

Academic & Awareness Programs

Metanoia: National seminar addressing issues like child trafficking...

CPR Training: A safety-focused event that provides staff with CPR skills...

Sports & Fitness

Sports Day: Annual athletic event where students from all departments participate...

Social & Community Engagement

The student council organizes blood donation camps, awareness drives, and community service programs...
